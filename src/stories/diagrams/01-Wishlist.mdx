# Wishlist — Feature Overview

Complete description of all user behavior variations: where data comes from, where it is saved, and what happens under which conditions.

---

## 1. Add and Remove from Wishlist

Entry points: heart button in the **catalog card** and on the **product page**.

<MermaidDiagram chart={`flowchart LR
    U([Click heart button]) --> B{Where from?}
    B -->|Catalog| C["ProductCard\\nhandleWishlist"]
    B -->|Product page| D["ProductDetailPage\\ntoggleItem onClick"]
    C --> E["toggleItem\\nuseWishlist"]
    D --> E
    E --> F{Already\\nin wishlist?}
    F -->|NO| G["addItem\\nadd to store"]
    F -->|YES| H["removeItem\\nremove from store"]
    G --> I["saveToStorage\\nlocalStorage"]
    H --> I
    G --> J["Heart: empty\\nbecomes filled"]
    H --> K["Heart: filled\\nbecomes empty"]`} />

**What is passed on add:** `id`, `name`, `brand`, `price`, `salePrice`, `image` (active color), `colors[]`, `colorStock[]`, `sizes[]`, `badge`, `inStock`, `selectedColor` (hex).
